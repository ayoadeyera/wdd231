About GoArt Collective

A cornerstone of the local art community since 2001

Our Mission

GoArt Collective serves as a digital bridge between local artists and art enthusiasts, providing a curated platform to discover, explore, and engage with contemporary visual art exhibitions in our community.

We believe in the transformative power of art to inspire dialogue, challenge perspectives, and create meaningful connections between diverse communities.

Our Story

2001 - Foundation

GoArt Collective was founded by a group of passionate art enthusiasts committed to creating a space where contemporary art could thrive.

2010 - Expansion

Expanded our gallery space to accommodate larger installations and increased our focus on digital art and mixed media exhibitions.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Launched our online platform to make art more accessible during challenging times, reaching audiences worldwide.

2026 - Present

Continuing to showcase groundbreaking exhibitions and foster the next generation of contemporary artists.
