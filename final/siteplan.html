Why This Project?

I chose this subject because of my personal interest in visual arts. This project offers rich opportunities to practice CSS styling and layout with high-quality imagery, while maintaining a clear, manageable three-page structure perfect for practicing information organization and content hierarchy.

Core Mission

GoArt Collective serves as a digital bridge between local artists and art enthusiasts, providing a curated platform to discover, explore, and engage with contemporary visual art exhibitions in our community.

Target Audience

Understanding who we're designing for

Art Enthusiasts

Individuals passionate about discovering new artists and attending gallery exhibitions. They seek curated experiences and appreciate detailed artist backgrounds.

Local Artists

Creators looking for exhibition opportunities and ways to showcase their work to a wider audience within the community.

Art Collectors

Buyers interested in emerging talent and investment-worthy pieces. They value detailed exhibition information and artist credentials.

Cultural Tourists

Visitors seeking authentic local art experiences. They need clear location information, hours, and current exhibition details.

Design System

A sophisticated palette inspired by premium brands

Color Palette

Wireframes

Responsive layouts for all devices

Desktop Layout

Optimized for large screens with multi-column layouts, prominent imagery, and advanced navigation patterns. Features a clean hero section, exhibition grid, statistics showcase, and comprehensive footer.

Tablet Layout

Balanced approach with stacked sections and touch-optimized interactions. Maintains readability while adapting content for medium-sized devices.

Mobile Layout

Single-column, mobile-first design with hamburger navigation and stacked content. Prioritizes essential information and easy thumb navigation.
